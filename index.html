EMERGENCY GO BAG ESSENTIALS


Water/Nonperishable food

    You need at least one gallon of water per person per day for three days, plus nonperishable food items.

First Aid Kit

    A basic first aid kit should include adhesive bandages, antiseptic wipes, gauze pads, and medical tape.


Radio

    A radio is important as it can relay important information.
